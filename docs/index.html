Privacy Policy for Sometimes

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

Sometimes ("the App") is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We believe in transparency about what data we collect and how it's used.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

All data generated by the App stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. We have no servers, no analytics, and no tracking.

Data Storage

What's Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device using Apple's secure storage:

  • Poem Delivery History: Records of which poems you've received and when
  • Favorites: Poems you've marked as favorites
  • Preferences: Your settings including:
    • Delivery frequency (poems per week)
    • Active hours (when poems can arrive)
    • Pause status (if you've paused delivery)

This data:

  • Remains on your device
  • Is never uploaded to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • Can be deleted by deleting the App
  • Is backed up to iCloud if you have App backup enabled in your device settings

What's NOT Stored

We do not collect or store:

  • Your name or email address
  • Your location or precise coordinates
  • Your contacts or photos
  • Any personal identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, etc.)

Network Usage

Sometimes uses the internet for these anonymous purposes only:

1. Weather Information

  • Service: Apple WeatherKit
  • What we send: Approximate location (city-level, derived from IP address via ipinfo.io)
  • What we receive: Current weather conditions
  • Privacy: No personal identifiers are sent. Location is approximate and temporary (not stored)
  • Purpose: To select contextually appropriate poems

2. In-App Purchases (Optional)

  • Service: Apple StoreKit
  • What's processed: Payment transactions for optional tips ("Small Coffee", "Nice Lunch", "Generous Support")
  • Privacy: All payment data is handled by Apple. We never see your payment information
  • Purpose: To support continued development (100% optional)

Third-Party Services

Apple Services We Use

  1. WeatherKit (Weather data)
    • Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
    • We don't control how Apple processes weather requests
  2. StoreKit (In-app purchases)
    • Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
    • We receive no payment information
  3. User Notifications (Poem delivery)
    • Managed by iOS
    • No data leaves your device

IP Geolocation Service

We use ipinfo.io to convert your IP address to an approximate location (city-level) for weather lookups.

  • What's sent: Your IP address (automatically sent by any internet request)
  • What's received: City and country
  • Privacy: No personal identifiers sent
  • Retention: Not stored by us; ipinfo.io has their own privacy policy
  • Frequency: Only when weather data is refreshed (approximately every 6 hours while app is in use)

Permissions We Request

Notifications

  • Why: To deliver poems at scheduled times
  • What we access: Permission to show notifications
  • Data shared: None - notifications are generated locally on your device

No Other Permissions

We do not request:

  • Location services
  • Camera or photo library
  • Contacts
  • Microphone
  • Calendar or reminders
  • Health data

Children's Privacy

Sometimes does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The App can be safely used by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing an in-app notice if changes are significant

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All data is on your device in the App
  • Delete your data: Delete the App to remove all data
  • Opt out of network features: Use the App offline (poems will still be delivered from the local library, but without weather-based selection)

Data Security

  • All data on your device is protected by iOS security measures
  • Network requests use HTTPS encryption
  • No data is transmitted to third-party advertising or analytics services

In Plain English

Sometimes is a simple poetry app that respects your privacy. All your favorites and settings stay on your phone. We only use the internet to get weather info (so we can pick fitting poems) and to process optional tips if you choose to support us. We don't track you, sell your data, or even know who you are. Enjoy the poetry. ❤️
