# Sometimes

> Poetry delivered at meaningful moments.

Sometimes is an iOS app that delivers carefully selected classic poems at random times throughout your day, matched to the context of the moment—the weather, season, time of day, and special occasions.

## Privacy

Sometimes respects your privacy completely:
- No data collection
- No tracking
- No analytics
- Everything stays on your device

[Read our Privacy Policy →](https://oddyblue.github.io/Sometimes-Poetry-App/)

## Features

- **Smart Delivery**: Poems arrive at meaningful moments, influenced by weather, season, and time
- **Beautiful Design**: Minimal interface that puts poetry first
- **Your Archive**: Every poem saved with favorites
- **Complete Control**: Set frequency, active hours, or pause anytime
- **No Ads**: Completely free with optional support tips
